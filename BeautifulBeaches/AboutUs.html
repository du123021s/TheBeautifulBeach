THE
BEAUTIFUL BEACHES

Enjoy but must preserve and protect.

The sea is a priceless treasure that mother nature has bestowed - rich, beautiful and pure.


BeautifulBeaches is simply a place to spread those beauties to everyone, double the love of beauty, and bring the beautiful scenes that created the miracle of creation to everyone.
For BeautifulBeaches, all the beaches on this planet have their own unique, interesting and romantic features. It is unique and an integral part of life. There is nothing more wonderful than a sweltering summer afternoon bathing in the crystal clear blue sea.
BeautifulBeaches as its name suggests is not only a place to provide information about the beautiful beaches on this planet but also a place to help you find the ideal sea that you can conquer on your next journey.
Finally, BeautifulBeaches wants to share the message "Love the sea, give the sea the best".

MISSION

In any field, to be successful, it is necessary to have a goal with clear tasks.



BeautifulBeaches will provide you with detailed information about great beaches throughout the Eastern, Western, Southern and Northern hemispheres of the Earth. What is unique about the beach such as outstanding features, diversity of ecological environment, rare marine animals, activities that you can participate in at the beach, food and transportation that you can refer to.
BeautifulBeaches always tries to find the most accurate information and images to provide to everyone. Depicting the truly heart-stopping beauty of the beaches. Let everyone fully enjoy the beauty of the sea at the nearest distance.
In addition to the above, BeautifulBeaches wants to arouse in people a sense of protecting the finite beauty of the beaches on the planet when tourism activities are gradually eroding these natural beauties. The pollution of the marine environment is the most obvious fact.

VISION

"The future depends on what we do in the future"
- Mahatma Gandhi -


In 2023, BeautifulBeaches aspires to become a popular search engine for beautiful beach information for everyone. Become a reliable tool and provide the most accurate information. Create a reputation as well as its position in the hearts of users.
Spreading the sea to let more people know, bringing the beauty of pristine beaches closer to everyone.
In 2024, BeautifulBeaches wishes to create real values in marine environmental protection. Partially improve the polluted marine environment.

Leave the sea what is most beautiful!

"Nature provides a free lunch, but only if we control our appetites"
- William Ruckelshaus -


The sea is a non-renewable resource. Things would be worse if the beaches on this planet became polluted. Nature has given us to enjoy the most beautiful things. We must behave worthy of we receive. Before the little green left of the Earth must be buried in the landfill.
